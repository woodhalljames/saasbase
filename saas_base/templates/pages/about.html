Our Mission

To eliminate the guesswork from wedding planning by giving couples the power to visualize their dream wedding before their special day arrives.

We understand that your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. That's why we've created tools that help you see, share, and celebrate your vision with confidence.

Our Story

DreamWedAI started when our founder was helping a friend plan their wedding. After visiting dozens of venues, they realized that couples were making one of their biggest decisions based on empty rooms and their imagination.

"What if we could show them exactly how their venue would look decorated?" That question sparked the creation of DreamWedAI - a platform that transforms any space into a wedding celebration.

Today, we're proud to help couples around the world visualize their perfect wedding day, share their vision with loved ones, and celebrate with confidence.
