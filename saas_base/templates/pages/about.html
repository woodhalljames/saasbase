We Believe Every Couple Deserves to Make Dreams Reality

DreamWedAI was born from a simple idea: wedding planning shouldn't be lengthy or expensive. We're here to help couples and wedding professionals alike visualize every step of the wedding journey.

Mission

To eliminate the guesswork from wedding and engagement planning by giving couples the power to visualize their dream venue or try on dresses and tuxedos before their special day arrives.

We understand that a wedding journey is one of the most important events in life. That's why we've created tools that help you see, share, and celebrate your vision with confidence.

Story

DreamWedAI began when our founder helped a friend plan their wedding. After visiting dozens of venues, struggling with uninspired engagement photos, and worrying about wedding day mishaps, we realized that creating something beautiful often comes with unnecessary risk.

To reduce that risk, we asked, “What if we could show couples exactly how their venue design, engagement photos, or wedding day would look with X,Y or Z?” That question sparked the creation of DreamWedAI, a platform that transforms any space into a wedding celebration, creates stunning engagement photos with just a selfie, and fixes or recreates problematic wedding day photos — and much more!

Today, we're proud to help couples and wedding professionals around the world create actionable, inspiring visuals using the best visualization and AI technology available.

The Problem

From engagement photos to venue design, couples often face stress and uncertainty at every step of the wedding journey.

The Solution

Advanced AI visualization technology designed specifically for engagements and weddings; paired with expert planner insights and photographer guidance to produce stunning, actionable results.

The Result

Confident, happy couples and planners who save time, money, and resources while building their dream weddings.
